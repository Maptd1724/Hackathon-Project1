THOR ODINSON

Thor Odinson is the Asgardian God of Thunder
the former king of Asgard and New Asgard
and a founding member of the Avengers.
Thor joined the Avengers under the guidance of S.H.I.E.L.D.
and stood with them to stop the schemes of his own adopted brother.
